Three steps

From consultation to delivery.

A simple, clear process so you know what to expect.

1

Initial consultation

We start with a conversation about your vision, timeline, and what matters most. I learn your story, venue, and style so the film reflects who you are as a couple.

2

Your wedding day

I capture everything—from getting ready to the last dance. I work unobtrusively so real moments unfold naturally while we get all the important details.

3

Editing & delivery

I review footage, sync audio, and craft your story. Within 6–10 weeks you'll get your film via a private online gallery to download and share.

My filming approach

Your film should feel like you—not a staged production. I give just enough direction for comfort, then step back so real moments unfold.

  • StyleCinematic, candid, story-driven
  • CoverageGetting ready through reception
  • AudioMic'd vows, speeches, key moments
  • DeliveryOnline gallery, easy sharing

What makes it different

I coordinate with your photographer so we're a team, not competing for attention. I focus on authentic moments and clean audio—the details that make a film you'll watch again and again.
