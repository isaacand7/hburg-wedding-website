What it's like to work with me

My filming approach

I believe your wedding film should feel like you—not like a staged production. I give just enough direction to make you feel comfortable, then step back so real moments can unfold.

  • Style Cinematic, candid, story-driven
  • Coverage Getting ready through reception (customizable)
  • Audio Mic'd vows, speeches, and key moments
  • Delivery Online gallery with easy sharing & downloads

I coordinate closely with your photographer so we're working as a team, not competing for your attention all day.
